Trip Planner Application

Manual-style sorting and pallet allocation based on kg.

PriorityNon-SL1 transactions are moved to the top first.
SortingAfter Non-SL1: qty/weight small → large, while keeping the same product together where possible.
Pallet LogicQty is KG. Pallets are packed by pallet weight capacity, not every row as one pallet.
LimitsMax pallet/trip and max weight/trip are enforced. Rows are never split.
